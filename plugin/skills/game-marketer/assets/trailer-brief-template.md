## Edit principles
- **Cut on the beat** of the music; never against it
- **Build, build, drop** structure (most game trailers)
- **No more than 3 text overlays** (text fights the visual)
- **Logo at end, not start** (the game is the hero, not the studio)
- **Subtitle-friendly** — many viewers watch muted

## Performance gates

- **Watch-through rate (full)** target: 30%+ on Steam, 20%+ on YouTube
- **Click-through (CTA)** target: see soft-launch creative plan
- **Hook drop-off** at 5s: <50% (a strong hook keeps most viewers past the first 5)
