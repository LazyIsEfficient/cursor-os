# Interview checklist

For each template, these are the questions to ask the user when the relevant section is missing or vague. Batch them into one AskUserQuestion call. Skip any whose answers are already obvious from the user's initial message or context.

## Universal questions (any template)

- **Audience**: "Who is the target audience? Role, company size, industry, pain point."
- **Funnel stage**: "Where in the journey — awareness, consideration, or decision?"
- **Success metric**: "What's the one number that tells you this worked? What's the current baseline?"
- **Existing assets**: "What do we already have to work with — content, data, lists, brand guidelines?"
- **Timeline**: "Is there a launch date, or is this open-ended?"
- **Out of scope**: "What should I explicitly *not* touch or expand into?"

## Campaign (`campaign-template.md`)

- **Channels**: "Which channels are in scope — email, organic social, paid, podcast, blog, landing pages?"
- **Core message**: "In one sentence, what's the single idea this campaign communicates?"
- **Budget**: "Is there paid spend, or is this organic only?"
- **Attribution**: "How do you track success today — UTMs, CRM, pixel, manual?"

## Content (`content-template.md`)

- **Format**: "What's the deliverable — X thread, LinkedIn post, blog, deck, newsletter, podcast repurpose?"
- **Core angle**: "What's the one takeaway the reader should leave with?"
- **Source material**: "Do we have anything to work from — a transcript, draft, data set, competitor example?"
- **Voice**: "Any brand voice guidelines, reference posts I should match, or specific tone?"
- **Quality gate**: "Should the output go through expert panel scoring before finalizing?"

## Optimization (`optimization-template.md`)

- **What's underperforming**: "Which specific asset, page, or sequence needs improvement? URL or path?"
- **Current numbers**: "What's the current metric and where does it need to be?"
- **Data available**: "What data do we have — analytics, heatmaps, session recordings, prior test results?"
- **Variants**: "How many variants do you want to test? Any constraints on what can change?"
- **Off-limits**: "What must stay the same — brand elements, layout, offer?"

## Research (`research-template.md`)

- **The actual question**: "What's the one question you want answered? Not 'tell me about X' — a question with an answer."
- **Decision it unblocks**: "What will you do differently depending on the answer?"
- **Competitors / channels**: "Which competitors or channels should I analyze?"
- **Depth**: "Quick scan, medium dig, or thorough? How much time should I spend?"
- **Prior knowledge**: "What do you already know or suspect? What have you ruled out?"

## Pipeline (`pipeline-template.md`)

- **Current state**: "New pipeline build, or tuning an existing one?"
- **Tools**: "What's already set up — Instantly, RB2B, HubSpot, Gong, LinkedIn, other?"
- **Deal size**: "What's the current deal size range, and where do you want it?"
- **Bottleneck**: "Where does the pipeline break — not enough leads, bad qualification, low close rate, or churn?"
- **Compliance**: "Any sending constraints — CAN-SPAM, GDPR, domain warming, daily send limits?"

## Question hygiene

- Never ask more than ~6 questions in one batch.
- Never ask a question whose answer is obvious from the user's message or context.
- Prefer concrete questions ("which channels?") over open-ended ones ("tell me about your marketing").
- If the user already volunteered something in prose, *distill it into the template* — don't ask them to repeat themselves.
- Always include the quality gate question unless the user has already specified whether they want expert panel scoring.
